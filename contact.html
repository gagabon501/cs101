Scam Alert

Please be aware that there are a growing number of fraudulent or misleading scams using the names of well-known retailers, including JB Hi-Fi. These can involve emails or SMS messages, phone calls, social media posts or other unsolicited promotions that use the JB Hi-Fi brand to request personal or financial information or direct you to “scam” websites that appear to be linked to the JB Hi-Fi. JB Hi-Fi does run legitimate competitions which can be found on the official JB Hi-Fi website or the JB Hi-Fi Facebook page. If a competition or special offer is not hosted on one of these official sites then it’s highly likely that it is a scam and you should not engage with it. If consumers have any doubt about a JB Hi-Fi competition or prize, they should contact JB Hi-Fi immediately at reception@jbhifi.co.nz
