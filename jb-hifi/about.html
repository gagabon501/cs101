About Us

JB Hi-Fi was established in 1974 by Mr John Barbuto (JB), trading from a single store in East Keilor, Victoria, Australia. He had one simple philosophy: to deliver a specialist range of Hi-Fi and recorded music at the lowest prices.

JB Hi-Fi rapidly grew in Australia and now has over 200 stores nationwide. JB Hi-Fi moved to New Zealand in 2007 with the acquisition of Hill & Stewart. There are now 14 stores throughout the country and is New Zealandâ€™s largest home and entertainment specialist.

With the original philosophy at the core of the business, JB stores offer the world's leading brands of computers, mobile phones, tablets, TVs, cameras, Hi-Fi, speakers, home theatre, portable audio and stacks more.

JB also offers the largest range of games, recorded music, DVD music + Blu-Ray and DVD movies and TV shows, all at cheap prices! Everything electrical you are after is available in one of our stores or online.

JB Hi-Fi has it all - best brands, huge range, cheapest prices, convenient locations, but most importantly genuine personal service from our experienced specialist staff.
